Description

Viña Alicia- Malbec 2013 by Viña Alicia is an exceptional guard wine, made in the gates, Luján de Cuyo, with an extended raising in oak that highlights its tertiary evolution. Ideal for hunting meats, truffled dishes or hard cheese, this malbec offers depth and sophistication. The 2013 vintage is at its peak, with an additional 5-7-year-old storage potential. Viña Alicia Malbec from Viña Alicia

Tasting Notes and Pairing

In the nose, it offers aromas to dry plum, leather and black truffle, with tobacco notes and exotic spices. In the mouth, it is deep, with flavors to ripe black fruits, bitter chocolate and licorice. The tannins are soft, with a balanced acidity. The end is long, with toasted coffee and minerality nuances. Marida with roasted deer, fungal risotto or old cheese.
