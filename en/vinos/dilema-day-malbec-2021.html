Description

Dilema Day Malbec is a young and versatile red wine from Dilema Wines, designed to be enjoyed without complications. Made in Mendoza, this Malbec reflects the freshness and fruity character of the region, ideal for relaxed moments, whether alone, with roast meats or vegetarian dishes. The 2023 vintage offers an accessible profile, perfect for casual meetings or informal dinners. Its relaxed style makes it an ideal option for any occasion.

Tasting notes & pairing

En nariz, presenta aromas a cereza, frambuesa y un toque de violetas, con matices herbales y una leve nota especiada. En boca, es ligero y jugoso, con sabores a frutos rojos frescos, ciruela y un dejo de pimienta negra. Los taninos son suaves, con una acidez vibrante que aporta frescura. El final es corto y limpio, con notas de fruta fresca. Marida con hamburguesas, empanadas de carne, pizzas o ensaladas con queso de cabra.
