Description

Dilema Day Malbec It is a young and versatile red wine of Dilemma Wines, designed to enjoy without complications. Made in Mendoza, this Malbec reflects the freshness and fruit character of the region, ideal for relaxed moments, either alone, with roasted meats or vegetarian dishes. The 2023 vintage offers an accessible profile, perfect for casual meetings or informal dinners. His decontracted style makes it an ideal option for any occasion. Dilema Day Malbec from Bodega Estancia Mendoza

Tasting notes & pairing

On the nose, it presents aromas to cherry, raspberry and a touch of violets, with herbal nuances and a slight spicy note. In the mouth, it is light and juicy, with flavors to fresh red fruits, plum and a black pepper. The tannins are soft, with a vibrant acidity that provides freshness. The end is short and clean, with fresh fruit notes. Marida with hamburgers, meat empanadas, pizzas or salads with goat cheese. Dilema Day Malbec from Bodega Estancia Mendoza
