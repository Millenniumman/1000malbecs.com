Description

Foster - Malbec 'Los Barrancos' is a premium wine from Enrique Foster, made with grapes from specific vineyards in Los Sauces, Valle de Uco, Mendoza. This Malbec stands out for its power and balance, with oak aging that highlights its structure. Ideal for red meats, spicy dishes or hard cheeses. The 2021 vintage offers aging potential of 8-10 years. Foster Malbec 'Los Barrancos' 2021 from Bodega Foster Lorca

Tasting notes & pairing

Vino fresco, joven y con características frutales. De color rojo intenso, con tonalidades violáceas. Posee aromas que rememoran frutos rojos frescos y pimienta blanca. Buena estructura y taninos suaves. Ideal para acompañar carnes blancas condimentadas, carnes rojas y comidas con salsas cremosas y lácteas. Excelente compañero del chocolate.
