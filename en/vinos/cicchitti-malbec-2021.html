Description

CICCHITTI Malbec 2015 is a guard wine Cicchitti, prepared in Maipú with an extended upbringing in French oak that highlights its tertiary evolution. Ideal for hunting meats, truffled dishes or hard cheese, this malbec offers depth and sophistication. The 2015 vintage is at its peak, with an additional guard potential of 3-5 years.

Tasting notes & pairing

On the nose, it offers aromas to leather, dry plum and black truffle, with tobacco notes and sweet spices. In the mouth, it is elegant, with flavors to ripe black fruits, bitter chocolate and licorice. The tannins are soft, with a balanced acidity. The end is long, with toasted coffee and minerality nuances. Marida with roasted deer, fungal risotto or old cheese. CICCHITTI Malbec from Cicchitti
