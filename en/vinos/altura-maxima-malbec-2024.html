Description

The true expression of the terroir in this Malbec comes from the extreme altitude at which the vineyard is located. This means that the grapes receive more sun and less UV protection, producing thicker-skinned berries that deliver a more robust palate, supported by fresh and vibrant acidity. The highest vineyard in the world gives us an extraordinary Malbec

Tasting notes & pairing

Notas intensas de bayas oscuras, ciruela, violeta, especias y toque mineral. Taninos finos, acidez refrescante, final aterciopelado y largo. Maridaje: carnes rojas premium, caza, quesos fuertes, platos con hongos.
