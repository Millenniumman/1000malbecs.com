Description

Italian word that means "to yearn." From Frances Mayes' memoir "Under the Tuscan Sun", the term "Bramare" captures the spirit with which Viña Cobos was founded. It symbolizes our first step in differentiating the appellations, to demonstrate its influence on the varietal. Express the identity of the origin.

Tasting notes & pairing

De color rojo rubí profundo con tonos violetas. Aromas a fruta roja, ciruela, grafito y notas florales. En boca es redondo y balanceado, con taninos dulces y jugosos.
