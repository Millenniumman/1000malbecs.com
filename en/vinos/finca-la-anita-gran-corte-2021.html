Description

He Finca La Anita Gran Corte 2021 It is a high-end wine from the winery Finca La Anita, located in Alto Agrelo, Luján de Cuyo, Mendoza, Argentina. This winery, founded in 1992 by Manuel Mas and acquired in 2016 by Swiss winemaker Bernard Fontannaz, is recognized as a pioneer in the production of premium wines in Argentina. The Gran Corte 2021 is a blend carefully prepared with grapes selected from the winery's own vineyards (72 hectares), located at 1100 meters of altitude in alluvial and clay soils, irrigated by the melting of the Andes. This wine reflects a modern, balanced and elegant style, combining the freshness of Malbec with the structure and complexity of other varieties, highlighting the quality of the Agrelo terroir.

Tasting notes & pairing

Violet red, with black highlights. A wine of great complexity; Modern in style, it is a blend where Malbec provides freshness, Cabernet Sauvignon its balsamic notes, Petit Verdot the structure and Cabernet Franc the sparkling and lively note. The nose expresses liquor and floral notes, with ripe red and black fruit. Firm and juicy tannins. Final length. We recommend serving with red meats, especially roasted or stewed. It goes very well with mature, fragrant or spicy cheeses, such as Camembert, Brie or Manchego. Ready to consume, recommended storage time 6 years. Finca La Anita Gran Corte 2021 from Finca La Anita
