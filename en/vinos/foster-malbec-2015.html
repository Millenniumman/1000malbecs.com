Description

Foster - Malbec is an emblematic red wine of Enrique Foster, prepared in Finca Los Beginates, Vista Flores, Uco Valley, with an focus on the elegance and expression of the Mendoza Terroir.This Malbec combines ripe fruit with a raising in French oak, ideal for red meats, pasta with intense sauces or halfway cheeses.The 2022 vintage offers structure and a 5-7-year-old guard potential.
