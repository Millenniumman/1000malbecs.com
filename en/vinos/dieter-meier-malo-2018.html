Description

Malo is produced using a selective technique for wine making, called microvinification, where each barrel is controlled at all times. We begin this process, completely artisanal, choosing the best grapes from our special lots. We then ferment the wine in small batches inside special containers at controlled temperatures, before aging the wine, and rotating each barrel by hand. The result? Exotic, elegant and complex: a wine like no other.

Tasting notes & pairing

Color: Rojo oscuro e impenetrable. Aroma: Multicapas y profundo en aroma, con flores de violeta, madera de cedro, ciruelas secas y tabaco. Sabor: Muy corpulento y rico en el paladar, con taninos de grano fino, extracto jugoso y con carácter, notas minerales, compacto y estructurado de manera coherente. ¡Un gran vino con gran potencial de guarda!
