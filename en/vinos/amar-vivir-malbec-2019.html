Description

Amar Vivir Malbec by Finca & Bodega Arca Yaco is a fruity and modern red wine, made in Cafayate with a focus on the purity of high-altitude Malbec. This wine celebrates life with its fresh and vibrant style, ideal for grilled meats, pastas with tomato sauces or cold cuts. The 2023 vintage stands out for its balance and accessibility, perfect for moments of everyday enjoyment.

Tasting notes & pairing

En nariz, presenta aromas a cereza, ciruela y un toque de especias dulces, con notas de violetas y un dejo herbal. En boca, es equilibrado, con sabores a frutos rojos, mermelada de frutilla y un toque de cacao. Los taninos son suaves, con una acidez vibrante. El final es redondo, con matices de fruta fresca. Marida con choripán, pastas al pomodoro o fiambres artesanales.
