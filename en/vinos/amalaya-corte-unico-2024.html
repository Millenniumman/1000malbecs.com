Description

Born in rocky alluvial soil, it is the icon wine of the winery; It is intense and seductive, and has an exquisite and memorable flavor, which reflects the great potential of northern Argentina. Fermented in barrels and French oak for 12 months, it is a wine of unique and admirable nature.

Tasting notes & pairing

Intense aromas and flavors of spices, floral notes, ripe fruits, jams, chocolate. Great mid-palate concentration, soft tannins, persistent finish. Pairing: grilled red meats, intense stews, game, aged cheeses.
