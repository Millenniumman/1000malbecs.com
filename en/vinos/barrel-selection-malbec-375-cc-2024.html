Description

This Malbec Barrel Selection from Salentein elegantly expresses the purity of the Uco Valley. Deep and intense purple-red in color, the nose displays a complex aromatic explosion: ripe red fruits, blackberries and black blueberries in the foreground, accompanied by subtle floral notes of violet that provide freshness. The palate is sweet and enveloping, with juicy and vibrant fruit that is balanced by refreshing acidity and elegant touches of aging in French barrels (vanilla and soft spices). Medium-high body, broad on the mid-palate and with polished tannins that lead to a long, persistent and balanced finish. A versatile, modern and very expressive wine from the Mendoza terroir, ideal for roasted meats, pastas with intense sauces or simply to enjoy alone.

Tasting notes & pairing

Presenta un color rojo púrpura muy intenso. En nariz, es intenso y complejo, con aromas destacados a frutos rojos, bayas negras, así como delicadas notas florales. En boca, su entrada es dulce y suave. Es un vino frutado y fresco con buena intensidad. En el medio de boca es amplio, con un final persistente y prolongado.
