Description

The idea of an alliance between the Rothschild and Catena families was born in 1999. With a winemaking tradition that goes back four generations and a With vast knowledge of the high-altitude terroirs of Mendoza, the Catena family is recognized as one of the most renowned Malbec producers. in Argentina. For its part, Domaines Barons de Rothschild (Lafite) provides a precious legacy of knowledge in the production of Cabernet Sauvignon. The objective of this alliance is to make a wine that combines the French and Argentine cultures and the two emblematic strains of both families. Aruma means 'night' in Quechua, the language of the Incas. This name expresses the intensity of the Andean nights and the depth of color that characterizes Malbec. Aruma Malbec 2023 from Bodegas Caro

Tasting notes & pairing

Intense red color with black reflections. The nose is expressive, with aromas of berries, plum and black currant. On the palate, the wine feels round and velvety, with aromas of blueberry, blackberry and cassis, leading to a smooth and balanced finish. Aruma Malbec 2023 from Bodegas Caro
