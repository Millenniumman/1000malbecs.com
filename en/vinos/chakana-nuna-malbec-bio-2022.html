Description

Coming from our organic and biodynamic vineyard at Finca Nuna, Agrelo, Luján de Cuyo, Mendoza, this 100% Malbec wine begins spontaneous fermentation thanks to its indigenous yeasts. With natural acidity, without clarification: vegan certification. Our Nuna Malbec in the glass appears intense, with a violet red color and medium depth. On the nose it expresses its simple, floral and fruity character. In the mouth you can feel its fresh natural acidity, its juicy red fruit profile and its medium persistence. That is why we recommend our organic and biodynamic Malbec for those palates that enjoy reds with medium body and complexity, with a fruity profile to accompany various dishes.

Tasting notes & pairing

En nariz, ofrece aromas a mora, cassis y hierbas frescas, con un toque mineral y notas de violeta. En boca, es equilibrado, con sabores a frutos negros, especias dulces y un dejo de grafito. Los taninos son suaves, con una acidez viva que aporta frescura. El final es persistente, con matices terrosos. Marida con berenjenas asadas, filete de cerdo o ensaladas con queso de cabra.
