Description

It was born from the personal cellar of Don Enzo Bianchi, who made some personal wines and shared them with friends and family. His motivation to launch this line on the market was accompanied by the insistence of his loved ones to enjoy it year after year. This is how Bianchi Particular was born, always maintaining the spirit of its origin

Tasting notes & pairing

Este Malbec se elabora con uvas de Finca Asti, Las Paredes, San Rafael, donde se obtienen uvas con muy buena concentración y tipicidad. De color rojo profundo y tonos violáceos. En nariz, notas de frutos negros, ciruela madura y notas especiadas, amalgamadas con reminiscencias de chocolate negro, vainilla y ahumados obtenidos de su crianza en roble. En boca presenta taninos amables, carnoso, elegante, de acidez equilibrada, con un final persistente y armónico
