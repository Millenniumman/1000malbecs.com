How to Buy

Buying your favorite wine at 1000malbecs is very simple:

  1. Choose your wine: Explore our selection of wines and pick your favorite.
  2. Click 'Buy': On each wine's page, you’ll find a 'Buy' button that will redirect you to WhatsApp.
  3. Contact via WhatsApp: Send us your order via WhatsApp .
  4. Pay for your order: You can pay via PayPal or bank transfer. We’ll provide the details when confirming your order.

Shipping Policy

At 1000malbecs.com, we want you to enjoy our wines, so we strive to keep a simple shipping policy that covers delivery costs without affecting our customers. We charge €5.99 per shipment, which allows us to verify age upon delivery. Alcohol is not sold to individuals under 18 years of age.

There is no minimum purchase amount. You can buy just one bottle, and it will be delivered to your doorstep.

Purchases of more than 11 bottles include free shipping.

Orders are generally dispatched within 24 hours after payment is received. If a product is temporarily out of stock, you will be notified when we receive your order.

You can pick up your order free of charge at our locations in Kaarst and Düsseldorf.

Bars and Restaurants

We aim to showcase our wine offerings to bars and restaurants. Please send us a message via WhatsApp, and we will get in touch with you. We offer exclusive volume discounts and events such as tastings so your customers can experience authentic Argentine Malbec firsthand.
