How to Buy

Buying your favorite wine at 1000malbecs is very simple:

  1. Choose your wine: Browse our selection of wines and pick your favorite.
  2. Click 'Buy': On each wine’s page, you’ll find a 'Buy' button that directs you to WhatsApp.
  3. Contact via WhatsApp: Send us your order via WhatsApp at +49 151 5822 4728.
  4. Pay for your order: You can pay via PayPal or bank transfer. We’ll provide payment details when confirming your order.
