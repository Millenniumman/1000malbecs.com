History of Malbec

Malbec, originally from France, found its perfect home in Argentina. Since its arrival in the 19th century, it has become the country's wine emblem, thanks to unique soil and climate conditions.

Featured Regions

Discover Argentina's wine regions: Mendoza, known for its intensity; Salta, with its high-altitude vineyards; and Patagonia, with its unique freshness.
