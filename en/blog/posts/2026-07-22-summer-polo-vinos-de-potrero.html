World Cup is over… but the summer continues

The World Cup is over and summer continues. A different one that kept us busy following our favorite team and perhaps another one. Now that the ball has stopped rolling and we have free time again, we will surely catch up on a series or read a good book.

For all the fans who keep reliving the matches in bars, those who still think their team deserved another fate, and those who simply want to celebrate, we have great news: we have added the Malbecs from Vinos de Potrero to our catalog.

Vinos de Potrero – Nicolás Burdisso’s project

Vinos de Potrero is a winery located in the heart of the Valle de Uco, Mendoza — the most famous region for its Malbecs.

It belongs to Nicolás Burdisso, former player of the Argentine National Team.
