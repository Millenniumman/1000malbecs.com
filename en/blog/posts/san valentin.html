Why Do We Fall in Love?

What drives us to seek a partner? How can we attract someone we like?

While I don't have answers to those questions, it's worth remembering that it's the senses that draw us to another person. And the senses are closely linked to experiences, aromas, and flavors.

- Its intense, deep color attracts us
- The aromas it releases transport us somewhere special
- And finally, when we kiss it (taste it), we complete a unique experience for every palate.

In short, a great wine and a great partner are both hard to find.
