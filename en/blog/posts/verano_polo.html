Polo, Malbec and asado.
Yes, also in Berlin, Hamburg, Frankfurt or Düsseldorf.
In short, the opportunity to live a 100% Argentine experience in Germany.

This German summer (which started a little later this year) will never be the same. Enjoying one of the most spectacular sports in the world, watching those horses and riders trying to get the ball through the goalposts while drinking a great Malbec… is an unforgettable experience.

Doing it next to the glowing embers, with a choripán in hand, is simply next level.

And that’s exactly what we do at 1000 Malbecs: invite our German friends to live and breathe a little piece of our culture for a while. Because that’s what it’s all about.
