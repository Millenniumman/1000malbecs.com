On April 17th we celebrated Malbec World Day at the Argentine Consulate General in Frankfurt. It was a wonderful evening full of great conversation, cultural exchange and excellent wines.

Consul General (a.i.) Frederico Franceschini hosted a select group of guests, including the Consul General of Chile and German authorities. The local press covered the event:

→ Welt Malbec Tag in Frankfurt a.M. gefeiert

For the occasion we selected five very different Argentine Malbecs, showcasing the diversity and quality of our wine regions. Here is the summary of the evening:
