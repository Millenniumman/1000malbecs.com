About Bodega Foster Lorca

The story goes back to 2002, when Enrique Foster, amazed by Malbec, summons Mauricio Lorca to start walking together.The winemaker then takes care of the entire production under both brands from the winery located in Perdriel, department of Luj√°n de Cuyo.From that moment, Mauricio Lorca has innovation as a flag, betting on new ways of living and doing viticulture.From premium wines without wood, our winery is positioned as a representative of the avant -garde, increasingly following the path of sustainability and being faithful to the expression of the terroir.
