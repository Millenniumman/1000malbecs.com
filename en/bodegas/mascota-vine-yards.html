About Mascota Vine Yards

Vineyards Mascota is an Argentine Author Wines Premium winery, widely awarded worldwide. Recently, the Pascota Cabernet Sauvignon 2016 was distinguished as the “Best Red Wine in the World” in the prestigious Vinalies International 2018 contest. Vineyards pet wines reflect the passion with which they were conceived and the unique influence exerted by their region of origin, in Mendoza, Argentina. Its exclusive unique wines portfolio is present in more than 35 countries, such as Denmark, Sweden, Switzerland, USA, China, Laos, Brazil and the United Kingdom, among others.
