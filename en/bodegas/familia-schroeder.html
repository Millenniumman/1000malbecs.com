About Familia Schroeder

Bodega Familia Schroeder is the fruit of the effort of a family of entrepreneurs who made her dream come true.In 2001, Herman Heinz Teodoro Schroeder, son of German immigrants in Patagonia, decides to invest with their children in vineyards.One of the most southern wine areas in the world in which man transformed a desert into effort into green vineyards and a productive area.
