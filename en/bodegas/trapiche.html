About Trapiche

Since its inception in 1883, Bodega Trapiche is recognized for the incessant search for innovation. The understanding of the Earth as our most precious good and the use of the latest technologies to venture into unexplored places, make a cellar that challenges what we know. The winery was a pioneer in aspects such as the introduction of French strains, the import of French oak barrels and the introduction of wines from a micro-terroir. The exploration of the highest standards in the world of viticulture, has led to not only being one of the leading brands in exports of Argentina but also to be internationally distinguished in countless occasions. It has been recognized as "the most awarded Argentine winery in the world" by the WRW & S for the fourth consecutive year, was chosen by the prestigious English publication Drinks International between the “50 most admired wine brands”, becoming the only winery in Argentina to obtain five nominations: 2014, 2015, 2017, 2018 & 2019 and in 2019 it was awarded by Wine enthusias New World Winery ”.
