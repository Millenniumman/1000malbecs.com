About Rutini Wines

In 1885 Felipe Rutini Fonda Bodega La Rural in Coquimbito, Maip√∫, and marks the future of the winery until turning its wines into synonym of the highest quality. Following its innovative spirit, around 1924 the winery begins to plant vineyards in the Uco Valley.
