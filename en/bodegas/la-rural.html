About La Rural

Joining an ancestral productive approach with the constant desire to combine innovative vision and evolution, the wines of the rural are recognized as a successful and relevant brand of our time. The central objective is to create products of the highest international quality. The labels are exported to more than 30 countries, selling more than 1,200,000 boxes a year. The wines have competed throughout the planet, obtaining various recognitions and awards, at the same time as meritorious world press comments, for the case The Wine Spectator and The Wine Advocate. Advanced systems and equipment for the production and breeding of wine also allow satisfying the constant demand of a growing number of new markets.
