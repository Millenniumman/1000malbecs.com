Frequently Asked Questions

How do I buy at 1000Malbecs?

Buying your favorite wine at 1000Malbecs is very simple:

  1. Choose your wine: Browse our selection of wines and pick your favorite.
  2. Click 'Buy': On each wine’s page, you’ll find a 'Buy' button that directs you to WhatsApp.
  3. Contact via WhatsApp: Send us your order through WhatsApp. Contact us on WhatsApp
  4. Pay for your order: You can pay via PayPal or bank transfer. We’ll provide the details when confirming your order.

Why can’t I buy directly on 1000Malbecs?

Malbec wine is relatively new in Germany, and we believe it’s important for purchases to be assisted by specialists to enhance the experience. As a startup, before launching an online solution, we’re keen to connect directly with our customers. We aim for a social commerce experience, where customers are supported by qualified staff before, during, and after the purchase to ensure you choose the wines that best suit your needs.

Will I be able to buy online in the future?

Absolutely! We’re working on an e-commerce solution and will soon offer wines through various channels. In the meantime, WhatsApp orders function like an online order, with the added benefit of allowing us to get to know you and provide personalized advice before you make your decision.

Can I become a social seller for 1000Malbecs?

Of course, we’re looking for wine enthusiasts and experts to join us in this mission. Please contact us via WhatsApp to learn about the sales model and how you can become a social seller of our wines. Contact us on WhatsApp

How can I sell wines on 1000Malbecs?

If you’re a wine importer in Germany and want us to feature your wines, please send us a message via WhatsApp, and we’ll get in touch promptly to explore collaboration opportunities. Contact us on WhatsApp

Shipping Policy

At 1000Malbecs, we want you to enjoy our wines, so we keep our shipping policy simple. We charge 5.99 EUR per shipment, which allows us to perform age verification upon delivery. No alcohol is sold to individuals under 18.

There is no minimum purchase amount. You can buy just one bottle, and it will be delivered to your address.

Orders of 12 or more bottles qualify for free shipping.

Orders are typically dispatched within 24 hours of payment receipt. If a product is out of stock, you’ll be notified upon order confirmation.

You can pick up your order free of charge at our points of sale in Kaarst and Düsseldorf.
